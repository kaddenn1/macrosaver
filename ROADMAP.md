# The Rule

Every feature must answer one question:

> **Does this help someone make a smarter buying decision?**

If the answer is no...

It doesn't get built.

---

# Definition of Success

MacroSaver succeeds when someone shopping for supplements thinks:

> **"I'm checking MacroSaver before I spend my money."**

Everything on this roadmap exists to make that happen.
