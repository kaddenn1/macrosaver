# Visual North Star

The homepage mockup stored in `/design/homepage-v1.png` is the visual source of truth for MacroSaver.

Every future design decision should move closer to this vision.

Do not redesign the visual language without founder approval.

Build upon it.

Do not replace it.

Core design principles:

- Premium dark interface
- Bold typography
- Green accent color
- Category-first shopping
- Large product imagery
- Data-first comparisons
- Spacious layouts
- Mobile-first responsiveness
- Trust over marketing
- Clarity over clutter

Every page should feel like it belongs to the same product family.

When in doubt, open `/design/homepage-v1.png`.

That image is the North Star.
